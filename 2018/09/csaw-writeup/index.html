  关于LDAP：


  LDAP的语法如下：

| Metachar | Meaning|
|———-|———-|
|& | Boolean AND|
| | | Boolean OR|
|! | Boolean NOT|
| = | Equals|
| ~= | Approx|
| = | Greater than|
| <= | Less than|
| * | Any character|
| () | Grouping parenthesis|

  所以LDAP表达and需要用(&(condition1)(condition2))，or类似。同时它还支持正则语法主要是：.、*。
